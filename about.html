About me

Hello!

My name is Cristina (aka Cree) Lester, I’m a developer with 8+ years of experience with front-end/back-end web development, performance analysis and optimization, site reliability, system management, web design, illustration and more. I’ve been working for different companies and as a freelancer using diverse platforms, tools, and languages. I enjoy being challenged and working on projects that require me to step outside of my comfort zone. My number one skill is problem solving which I exercised through the years by debugging infinite lines of code. I'm a quick learner. Whatever comes up, I'll figure it out! I'm also a bass player, digital and analogic illustrator, gourmet cook, weight lifter, yoga student and Muay Thai kickboxer.
